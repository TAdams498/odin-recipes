Dumplings with Sides

Description

Using Assi brand vegetable dumplings found at Lotte Plaza, this dish is quick and very savory. I eat mine with a soy sauce mix that I prepare, and I also try to include some sides as well to balance out the meal.

Ingredients

  • Assi brand vegetarian dumplings
  • Corn starch
  • Olive oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Rice wine vinegar
  • Scallions
  • Thai chiles
  • Sugar
  • Kimchi
  • Pickled cabbage
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat
  2. When oil is warm, add as many frozen dumplings as desired, flat side down, and cover the pan, check periodically for golden brown underside
  3. In a small bowl, add a drop of sesame oil, it's important not to put too much otherwise the flavor will be overwhelmed
  4. Add soy sauce to the bowl, and a tiny bit of rice wine vinegar
  5. Stir in a spoonful of sugar to the sauce mix until dissolved, add more sugar if a thicker sauce is desired
  6. Chop up the thai chiles and scallions and mix them into the sauce bowl
  7. Mix corn starch and water into a small bowl to make a slurry
  8. When dumplings are golden brown on the bottom, add slurry to pan. Make sure it is evenly spread over the bottom of the pan and touching all of the dumplings
  9. Once corn starch mix has solidified, and the bottom of the dumplings looks brown enough, remove from pan
  10. Serve dumplings with sauce, rice, kimchi, and pickled cabbage on the side
